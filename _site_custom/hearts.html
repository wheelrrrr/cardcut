Play

Hand

Deal

Random dealer. 17 (3) , 13 (4) or 10 (5 [2♦] & [2♣] removed) cards each.

Pass Cards

All players choose 3 cards to pass face down.

Pass Rotation

On 1st hand: pass to left, 2nd hand: pass to right, 3rd hand: pass opposite, 4th hand: no passing.

Play

Player with 2♣ leads by playing it (or 3♣ if no 2♣).

Trick

Lead any suit but not Hearts ♥ until a Heart has been played ('broken').

Each Player

Follow Suit

Play card following suit if possible.

Play any other suit

Including Heart ♥ and Black Maria Q♠.

Next Trick

Winner of Trick is highest rank of lead suit. Winner leads next trick until no more cards.

Score

Each Heart won is 1pt. Black Maria Q♠ is 13pts.

Shooting The Moon

If player wins ALL Hearts & Q♠ remove 26pts from their score or add 26pts to all others (player choice).

Next Hand

When all cards played.

Game End

A player reaches 100pts (or any value agreed). The player with the lowest score wins.
